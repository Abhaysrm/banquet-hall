All About Grand Rama Ceremonial

The Grand Rama Ceremonial was specifically made to host large-scale events and provide the same level of efficiency whether they are formal or informal in nature. We offer three amazing large halls that are decorated royally, with their walls adorned with flowers and matching decorations. The halls make for a large capacity of guests that can be accommodated at the venue. The wedding hall offers services that are ideal for any type of occasion.

The venue's royal looks and intricate details are among its unique features. It has big low-hanging chandeliers that provide amazing lighting to the place, its walls are either adorned with flowers or designed with amazing arches and ledges with intricate design work on them. The colors are also very exotic looking and provide for a one-of-a-kind photography experience. The banquet hall also offers a decor team to help our guests customize the venue decorations and have their tastes visualized on our grand canvas.

A good banquet is known for the quality of its services and the hospitality it provides, and we at Rama group of banquets strive to achieve the task to the fullest. Beginning with the sizable three halls that allow you to host events with a capacity of around 1500 guests, we also have an outdoor lounge to accommodate more guests and allow versatility to the events, outdoor events such as performances, talent shows, and games can be included in your programs as well. The banquet hall provides an amazing menu filled with exotic, tasty dishes for both veg and non-vegetarian guests. You can choose and customize the entrees and also the main dishes as well.

We also offer you an in-house event planner to help you coordinate the event more professionally. You can work along with them to customize almost every aspect of your occasion. All these amazing services are very easy to find, The Grand Rama Ceremonial is located in Greater Noida and is opposite CRPF Camp, Suthiyana. The banquet hall also offers valet service for your guests and has a parking capacity for up to 40 cars.

The Grand Rama Ceremonial is a venue available for hosting your wedding ceremonies. The wedding venue is one of the amazing decisions when it comes to planning a wedding, and it requires fulfilling a lot of expectations.
